About

Academic Systems Consulting partners with universities and tertiary institutions to enhance academic quality and operational effectiveness.
Led by a PhD-qualified higher education specialist, we bring deep expertise in learning analytics, academic governance, and IT-enabled business analysis.
Our approach is grounded in research and a commitment to the student experience.

What We Do

  • Learning Analytics & Surveys
    Design and interpret institution-wide surveys, learning analytics, and engagement data to inform strategy and improve outcomes.

  • Policy & Risk Analysis
    Review and strengthen policy frameworks, identify risk, and recommend pragmatic controls that align with regulatory compliance.

  • Business Process Improvement
    Map and analyse academic and administrative processes to reduce staff workload, streamline systems, and deliver measurable efficiency gains.

  • Automation & IT Project Support
    Plan and oversee automation initiatives, provide corrective input to IT projects, and ensure technology delivers on academic and operational needs.

  • Student Experience Consultation
    Facilitate student feedback processes and translate insights into actionable recommendations that enhance satisfaction and success.

Why Choose Us

  • Student-Centred: Every engagement keeps student learning and wellbeing at the core.
  • Evidence-Driven: Data analysis and academic research underpin all recommendations.
  • Collaborative: We work with academic and professional staff to co-create sustainable solutions.
  • Compliance Ready: Expertise in TEQSA, ESOS, and ASQA ensuring regulatory obligations are upheld.

Outcomes You Can Expect

  • Reduced administrative burden and clearer staff workload allocation.
  • Project rescue to identify next steps in stalled projects.
  • Realistic policies and processes that support strategic and academic goals.
  • Systems and dashboards aligned to the academic user experience.
  • Data-informed decisions that elevate the student experience.
