# Engagements

## Typical Project-Based Engagements

All project fees are in AUD and exclude GST.* Final fees depend on confirmed scope, complexity, timelines, and data quality.

### Research Design and Evaluation

- **Research Design Package**  
  - Problem definition and refinement  
  - Methodology and study design  
  - Instrument selection or development plan  
  - High-level analysis plan  
  - Fee: 7,500–12,000*

- **Program / Service Evaluation (Single Program)**  
  - Evaluation framework and logic model  
  - Data collection plan and tools  
  - Quantitative and/or qualitative analysis  
  - Final evaluation report and presentation  
  - Fee: 15,000–25,000+*

- **Psychometrics and Survey Development**  
  - Item drafting and refinement  
  - Pilot testing design  
  - Reliability and validity analysis  
  - Technical documentation  
  - Fee: 8,000–18,000*

### Data and Reporting

- **Data Analysis Only (Client Provides Dataset)**n  - Data review and analysis plan  - Statistical analysis and/or modelling  - Results summary and recommendations  - One feedback/clarification round  - Fee: 4,000–12,000*

- **Evidence Review and Recommendations**n  - Targeted literature / evidence scan  - Synthesis of key findings  - Practical recommendations for implementation  - Executive summary or slide deck  - Fee: 6,000–15,000*

- **Academic-Style Manuscript Support**n  - Methods and results drafting  - Tables, figures, and appendices  - Alignment with journal guidelines (content only)  - Fee: 6,000–15,000*

### Workshops and Capacity Building

- **Custom Training / Workshop Package**n  - Needs assessment and agenda design  - Custom materials and exercises  - 2–3 hour live workshop (online or in person, location dependent)  - Summary notes and recommendations  - Fee: 2,200–6,000*

---

### Discounts for Eligible Non-Profits

- Eligible non-profit and community organisations may receive up to **15%** discount on project-based fees, applied at the proposal stage.*

---

### Deposits and Payment Structure

- **Deposit:** 40–50% of the total project fee is payable upon acceptance of the proposal and before project commencement.*  
- **Milestone Payments:** Interim payments are tied to agreed deliverables (for example, upon delivery of design documents, draft reports, or workshops).*  
- **Final Payment:** Balance is due upon delivery of the final agreed deliverables*

---

### Additional Fees and Charges*

The following may apply depending on the project:

- Extended or additional stakeholder consultations beyond the agreed scope  
- Substantial changes in scope after project commencement  
- Complex data cleaning, restructuring, or integration of multiple datasets  
- Additional rounds of revisions beyond those specified in the proposal  
- Travel time and expenses for in-person work outside Brisbane  
- Specialised software, data access, or transcription services (if required)

\* Full details, including any applicable discounts, will be confirmed in a written proposal and engagement letter.

# Ad Hoc Consulting and Retainer Rates

## Ad Hoc Consulting Rates

All ad hoc fees are in AUD and exclude GST.* Time is billed in 15-minute increments with a minimum 1-hour engagement for new clients.*

### Hourly Rates

- **Standard Consulting (strategy, design, evaluation, supervision):** 280/hour*  
- **Urgent or After-Hours Consulting (short notice, evenings, weekends):** 350/hour*  
- **Data Analysis and Technical Work (statistics, Python, Power Query, dashboards):** 260/hour*  
- **Document Drafting and Review (reports, ethics, protocols, grant sections):** 240/hour*  
- **Training / Workshop Delivery (short sessions, not packaged as a full engagement):** 300/hour*

### Specific Ad Hoc Items

- **Scoping / Discovery Session (remote, up to 2 hours including preparation):** 1,000*  
- **Stakeholder Interview or Focus Group (up to 90 minutes including preparation and brief notes):** 650–900 per session*  
- **Ethics or Governance Application Drafting (short form):** from 2,500*  
- **Instrument / Survey Build (e.g., Qualtrics, REDCap, SurveyMonkey):** 1,500–4,000*  
- **Data Cleaning and Documentation:** 220/hour*  
- **Visualisation and Dashboard Development:** 260/hour*  
- **In-Person Travel Time (beyond local area):** 180/hour plus agreed expenses*

Eligible non-profit and community organisations may receive up to **15%** discount on ad hoc hourly and fixed-fee items, subject to prior agreement.*

## Retainers

Retainers are suitable for organisations seeking ongoing access to advice, supervision, and light support, without commissioning a full project each time.

### Light Advisory Retainer

- Fee: 3,000 per month*  
- Inclusions:  
  - Up to 6 hours of advisory support per month (strategy calls, quick reviews, ad hoc questions)  
  - Email support with responses within 3 business days  
- Additional time billed at standard hourly rates.

### Standard Advisory Retainer

- Fee: 5,500 per month*  
- Inclusions:  
  - Up to 12 hours of advisory support per month  
  - Priority email support (response within 2 business days)  
  - One scheduled check-in meeting per month (up to 60 minutes)  
- Additional time billed at standard hourly rates.

### Premium Embedded Partner Retainer

- Fee: from 9,000 per month*  
- Inclusions:  
  - Up to 20 hours of advisory and light implementation support per month  
  - Priority response (within 1 business day where possible)  
  - Standing monthly or fortnightly strategy meetings  
- Additional time billed at a preferred partner rate (to be agreed).

---

### Retainer Payment and Terms

- Retainers are invoiced **monthly in advance** and payable within 14 days.*  
- Unused hours do not roll over unless otherwise agreed in writing.*  
- Project-based work that falls outside the agreed retainer scope will be quoted and billed separately.*  
- Eligible non-profit organisations may receive up to **15%** discount on retainer fees, agreed at contract stage*

\* Full terms, including scope, rates, discounts, and payment conditions, are set out in the individual proposal and engagement letter.
